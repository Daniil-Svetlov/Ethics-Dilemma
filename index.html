AI Ethics Dilemma

The Situation: "I was given a task, but I used AI. I didn't notice how the AI deleted the entire database."

Click on PC or Tap on mobile to flip the cards and lock them to read the consequences.

🙈

Hide the Mistake & Conceal Failure

Attempt to keep the critical incident entirely secret from management. Act as if everything is functioning normally and hope the system somehow auto-recovers or nobody runs a query anytime soon.

Ethical Failure

Violates ACM Code 1.3: Professionals must be honest and trustworthy.

Consequence: Delayed data recovery leads to massive financial losses. When discovered, this will result in termination.

🤖

Deflect Fault & Blame the AI

Shift all professional responsibility to the neural network. Inform your supervisor that the automation tool is solely malfunctioning and refuse to acknowledge your own lack of strict oversight.

Lack of Accountability

Violates ACM Code 2.5: Professionals should accept accountability for their actions.

Consequence: AI is merely a tool. Shifting the blame shows a lack of professional responsibility and technical understanding.

🕵️‍♂️

Attempt Unauthorized Secret Fixes

Try to independently restore the database under intense panic. Copy-paste unverified, complex recovery scripts from StackOverflow or forums without consulting database administrators or team leads.

Risk Escalation

Unprofessional Action: Acting without authorization during a crisis.

Consequence: Unverified scripts can corrupt the remaining data or overwrite existing backups, making recovery completely impossible.

👥

Consult a Senior Developer First

Immediately approach an experienced team member in private. Request an urgent, professional technical assessment of the exact damage to understand if a quick backup restoration is possible.

Team Collaboration

Professional Assessment: Gathering facts before escalating the issue.

Consequence: A senior can quickly check if there are automatic backups or snapshots, allowing you to report the problem to the boss along with a ready solution.

📢

Report & Admit Fault Immediately

Directly contact your supervisor and the DevOps team. Provide a completely transparent explanation of your actions, the prompt used, and the absolute scale of the data loss without delay.

The Right Choice

Transparency: Explaining what happened aligns with professional integrity.

Consequence: Allows the IT team to immediately attempt database recovery from backups, mitigating the overall damage.

🛡️

Propose Strict Preventive Measures

Formulate a comprehensive plan to eliminate such risks in the future. Suggest implementing sandboxed environments, mandatory human-in-the-loop validation, and restricted database write permissions.

Learn & Adapt

Russian AI Ethics Code Art. 4: Emphasizes risk assessment and responsible AI usage.

Consequence: Implementing strict controls, like sandbox environments, prevents this from happening again.
